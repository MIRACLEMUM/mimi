how to prepare afang soup

pay attention everyone

list of things you will need

  1. Money
  2. Afang
  3. waterleaf
  4. palm oil
  5. periwinkle (it optional)
  6. fish
  7. stockfish
  8. garri/fufu(me i love fufu)
  9. meat
  10. pepper,maggi,crayfish

    11. pay attention on how to prepare it

    it only miracle that will give you the right answers

    • cut the afang into small slice and grand it
    • wash your waterleaf and slice it into small piece
    • wash your meat,periwinkle,stockfish,and fish
    • season it and allow it cook for some minute
    • put your waterleaf into the pot allow it cook for 1 minute
    • pour your seasoned meat,fish,stockfish,palm oil and periwinkle into the pot
    • allow it cook for 3 miunte
    • then you add your pepper,knor seasoning(i love using knor to cook)and crayfish
    • then you cover your pot and allow it cook
    • after 2 minute open your pot and add alittle water (it optional)
    • why i said water is optional is because the waterleaf will bring out alot of water
    • if you add water then wait for it to boil then you add your afang
    • allow it bowl for 2 minute
    • then you bring your pot down from the fire
    • then you bowl water for your garri (i prefer fufu)
    • now your delicious afang soup is ready to be served
